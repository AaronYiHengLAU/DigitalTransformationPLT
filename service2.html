🎯 Different Types of Displays

Not sure which display is right for you? We work with all the major types, and we'll help you pick the one that fits your needs and budget.

  • Smart TVs - Great for offices and homes, connect to WiFi and stream anything
  • LED Displays - Super bright screens perfect for storefronts and outdoor use
  • LCD Monitors - Reliable workhorse displays for everyday business use
  • OLED Screens - Amazing picture quality with deep blacks, perfect for home theaters
  • Video Walls - Multiple screens working together for big impact presentations
  • Touch Displays - Interactive screens that respond to finger touches

🧠 What Makes Smart TVs Smart

Smart TVs are basically regular TVs with built-in internet. They're really convenient - no need for extra boxes or cables for streaming.

  • Built-in WiFi so you can stream Netflix, YouTube, and other apps directly
  • Works with Alexa and Google Assistant for voice control
  • You can mirror your phone or laptop screen wirelessly
  • Gaming mode reduces lag if you're playing video games
  • Great picture and sound quality with HDR and Dolby features
  • Can control other smart home devices from the TV interface
  • Regular software updates add new features over time

📊 Picture Quality Options

Confused by all the resolution numbers? Here's what they actually mean and which one you probably need.

  • 4K - Four times sharper than regular HD, great for most people
  • 8K - Super crisp but honestly, most content isn't even available in 8K yet
  • HD - Still perfectly fine for smaller screens and basic use
  • LED displays - We can customize the sharpness based on viewing distance
  • HDR - Makes colors pop and dark scenes easier to see
  • 120Hz - Smoother motion, especially noticeable in sports and gaming
